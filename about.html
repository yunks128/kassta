About KASSTA

President's Message

From: Eun-Suk Seo, Ph.D.
Founding President, Professor at University of Maryland

KASSTA was established as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit in 2024 to strengthen U.S.-Korea collaboration in aerospace fields.

Recent Achievements

  • Monthly webinar series featuring astronauts, NASA scientists, and aerospace engineers
  • First AeroSpace Forum during UKC 2024 in San Francisco (August 2024)
  • Three-day workshop at Caltech in December 2024, jointly organized with Korean Space Science Society and NASA JPL, attracting 70 scientists

Future Plans

  • Second workshop in Korea (October 2025)
  • Third workshop in U.S. (August 2026)
  • Fostering partnerships in space exploration

Call to Action

Join KASSTA, take leadership roles, and participate in UKC 2025 in Atlanta (August 5-9).

Bylaws

Purpose

  • Advancing space and aeronautics through USA-Korea cooperation
  • Promoting professional fellowship
  • Cultivating the next generation of Korean-American scientists and engineers
