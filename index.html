About KASSTA

Bridging aerospace communities across the Pacific

KASSTA is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in 2024 to strengthen U.S.-Korea collaboration in aerospace science and technology. Through monthly webinar series, workshops, and forums, KASSTA provides a platform for Korean American aerospace professionals and researchers to share knowledge, foster innovation, and build lasting partnerships between the United States and South Korea.
